Facilities for Students

  Sports :The college offers sports facilities in various games. Students are supposed to actively participate in sports activities. Regular practice will make students eligible to get selected for various competitions. Students should contact the sports officer time to time and see notices on the notice boards to get information related to the schedule of practice.

  NSS / NCC :It is compulsory for the students of B. Sc. I and II to opt for either of the two activities. Minimum 75% attendance in them is imperative.

  • NSS :The college has one unit for boys and one for girls. Each unit accommodates hundred volunteers. N.S.S. includes social service rendered in he fields of civil defense, slums, construction work in rural areas through manual work, adult education, fight against social evils, nursing, blood donation, first aid etc. It is obligatory on participants to work for 240 hours in two years so as to obtain the certificate.
  • NCC :It has following units :
    • Infantry (53 Seats). Officer-Lt. Rajeev Dixit
    • Armed(65 Seats) Officer Capt. Sanjay Vyas
    • Armed Offers wing-Officer- Maj. Mrs. Preeti Chaturvedi
    • Air wing (200 seats for Indore city).

Career Guidance cell : The cell offers guidelines to students about professions of their choice and interest and lends guidance to students preparing for Junior Research Fellowship and other competitive examinations.

Bank and Dispensary :The college provides the facilities of bank ( a Branch of State Bank of Indore is Operating in the College Premises) and Govt. Dispensary to the Students, Professors and other Workers of the College.

Fund :University Relief Fund for handicapped students and students who meet with accident has been established by D.A.V.V.Indore. Regular college students are helped from the fund . Information regarding rules, eligibility, objectives, amount etc. applicable to both the schemes can be obtained from the College office or the Students Union convener in the university.

Hostel
  • Admission forms for hostel are available in the office of the hostel which should be filled and submitted in duplicate along with the admission forms meant for the college .
  • A student will be admitted in the hostel only after securing admission in college. Admission in hostel depends on academic merit, and excellent character certificate. The admission is given only for one academic session at a time. It is compulsory that at least one of the parents accompany their ward at the time of seeking admission in the hostel.
  • After getting admission in the hostel the student is required to deposit the fees for the whole year with the Cashier in the college office . The date of admission in hostel does not affect the amount of the fees.

Rules For Hostel
  • Only regular students of Holkar Science College are eligible for admission in the hostel. The admission to the hostel is completely subject to the discrimination of the concerned committee.
  • Any friend, relative or acquaintance will not be permitted either to visit the hostel or to stay in the hostel rooms, without specific reason.
  • Complete discipline must be maintained in the premises and other places. Well mannered and cordial conduct with other hostlers and workers must be observed. If found involved in ragging and in-disciplinary acts the student will be rusticated from hostel and college and legal action will be taken against him. Any objectionable events should be immediately reported to the warden.
  • It is compulsory to deposit the fees and mess-charges in due time. Any breach will result in rustication from the hostel.
  • The parents must keep in regular touch with the warden. They should also inform the warden about time amount which they regularly sent their wards to prevent any misuse of amount.
  • It is compulsory for all the hostlers to join the clubmess. Amount of Rs. 2950/- must be deposited in the account No. EX-H2 in State Bank of Indore College Extension Branch, at the time of admission and its receipt must be attached with the admission form. The amount of the mess-bill must also be deposited in the same account. Other amount of Hostel fees must be deposited in the office of the college hostlers will share water and electricity charges on the Co-operative basis.
  • It is prohibited to join coaching classes or tuition without the permission of the Principal.
  • A hostler can not remain absent from the hostel without the prior permission of the warden.
  • Safety of the supplied things, books and furniture is the responsibility of the hostler, for any loss or lose and damage Charges will be recovered from the hostler.
  • In case of any illness, it should be immediately reported to the warden.
  • The hostlers must stay in their rooms from 9 P.M. to 6 A.M.
  • Smoking Alcohol and drugs are strictly prohibited.
  • It is essential to seek the permission of the warden, to deposit the outstanding fee and supplied things before a hostler discontinues the hostel facilities.
  • 75% attendance in every subject in the college is compulsory for the hostler. In case of short attendance they shall be rusticated from the hostel .
  • Admission in the hostel in the middle of the session is not possible.
  • Admission in the hostel for one year does not guarantee admission for the next year.
  • It is essential for the hostler to operate radio and T.V. in low volume.
Violation of the above mentioned rules will result in immediate rustication from the hostel.
In case of any inconvenience the hostler should contact the warden. The warden will help to make the hostlers comfortable, smooth and ideal co-operation of the hostlers is also expected.
